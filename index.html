Kobe Bryant

Here is the Timeline of Kobe Bryant
  • Born on August 23, 1978 in Philadelphia, PA
  • With the 13th pick in the 1996 NBA Draft, the Charlotte Hornets selected Kobe Bryant
  • In 1997, Bryant was 18 The time All-Star Weekend rolled around. He didn’t make the team, but he did participate in the Rookie Challenge, and he won the Slam Dunk Contest.
  • From 1999-2002, The Lakers had been a playoff team with Bryant and Shaquille O’Neal, but they hadn’t quite reached championship form. The furthest they’d made it was to the conference finals.
  • In 2004, Bryant and O’Neal had butted heads since they first teamed up in 1996. Shaq took issue with the style of basketball Bryant played, labeling him “selfish” and a “show off.”
  • In 2016, Bryant spent the rest of his career chasing a sixth NBA title, which would have tied him with Michael Jordan. But injuries plagued him his final few seasons.
  • In 2017,the Lakers retired Bryant’s jersey numbers. He’d worn No. 8 until 2006, when he changed to No. 24. He’d first worn 24 in high school before changing to 33.
  • On Jaunary 26, 2020 marks the passing of legend Kobe Bryant.
